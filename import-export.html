Simplify Your Global Trade Operations

Navigating the complexities of international trade can be challenging, with ever-changing regulations, customs procedures, and logistics requirements. Fortusymbol provides professional goods import-export agency services to help businesses of all sizes streamline their global trade operations.

Drawing on our own experience as an international jewelry brand with extensive cross-border trade experience, we understand the unique challenges businesses face when importing and exporting goods. Our team of trade experts handles every aspect of the process, from documentation and customs clearance to logistics and delivery.

Whether you're importing raw materials from China, exporting finished products to global markets, or need assistance with trade compliance, we have the expertise and network to make your international trade operations smooth and efficient.

Our Import-Export Process

A streamlined 6-step process to ensure your goods move smoothly across borders

1

Consultation & Planning

  • Understand your shipment requirements
  • Provide cost & timeline estimates
  • Develop customized shipping plan
  • Identify regulatory requirements
2

Documentation & Preparation

  • Prepare all required documentation
  • Obtain necessary licenses & permits
  • Arrange cargo insurance
  • Coordinate with suppliers
3

Transportation & Customs

  • Arrange international transportation
  • File customs declarations
  • Handle customs inspections
  • Pay duties & taxes
4

Clearance & Delivery

  • Complete customs clearance
  • Arrange local transportation
  • Deliver to your facility
  • Provide delivery confirmation
5

Warehousing (Optional)

  • Store goods in secure facilities
  • Manage inventory levels
  • Fulfill customer orders
  • Provide inventory reports
6

Post-Shipment Support

  • Provide final documentation
  • Handle any post-shipment issues
  • Process insurance claims if needed
  • Continuous process improvement
